UniFed-leaderboard
A Benchmark for Federated Learning Frameworks

In UniFed leaderboard, with 15 evaluation scenarios, we present both qualitative and quantitative evaluation results of 9 existing popular open-sourced FL frameworks, from the perspectives of functionality, usability, and system performance. Please find more details in our paper here.

Evaluation Scenarios

UniFed borrow 15 datasets from existing works to cover different FL settings, modalities, task types, and workload sizes.
